About Us

New Look Painting QLD

New Look Painting QLD is a trusted name in the Australian painting industry, with over 15 years of experience delivering exceptional results for residential and commercial clients across Queensland and beyond.

Our team of fully qualified and licensed painters brings a meticulous approach to every project — from careful surface preparation to flawless final coats. We use only premium paints from Australia's leading brands to ensure lasting beauty and protection.

Whether you're refreshing the interior of your home, repainting your commercial premises, or tackling a large-scale industrial project, New Look Painting QLD delivers on time, on budget, and beyond expectations.
